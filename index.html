AI storytelling for kids

About
During the meeting at Hackgarden in 05/01/2023, we created this digital interactive book-experience for kids. The idea was using the webcam to recognize some drawings on paper and start generate stories using chat GPT.

For the input, we used the Teachable Machine to train our model with a deck of cards: https://teachablemachine.withgoogle.com/

Then, we exported our model in Keras file with the 6 classes.

An example

Tell a kid a story that has a CAR and the character is LITTLE GIRL and does it: Blow a house 
							Once upon a time, in a small town, there lived a little girl named Sophie. Sophie was a very curious and adventurous young girl who loved nothing more than going on exciting car rides with her parents.
							One day, Sophie and her parents decided to take a trip to the countryside to visit some of their old friends. As they were driving along the winding roads, they came across a beautiful old house nestled among the trees.
							Sophie's eyes lit up as she saw the house, and she begged her parents to stop the car so she could take a closer look. As they pulled up to the house, Sophie couldn't contain her excitement and she ran up to the front door.
							But as she got closer, she noticed that the house was in need of some serious repair. The roof was missing tiles, the windows were cracked, and the paint was peeling. Sophie's heart sank as she realized that the beautiful old house was in danger of falling apart.
							Determined to help, Sophie ran back to the car and grabbed her toolkit. She knew that she couldn't fix the house all on her own, but she was determined to try.
							Over the next few days, Sophie worked tirelessly on the house, patching up the roof, fixing the windows, and painting the walls. And as she worked, she began to see the house coming back to life.
							Finally, after weeks of hard work, the house was as good as new. Sophie stood back and gazed at her handiwork, a huge smile spreading across her face. She had saved the house and it was all thanks to her hard work and determination.
							From that day on, Sophie became known as the "little girl who blew a house," and she was celebrated throughout the town for her bravery and determination. And every time she drove past the beautiful old house, she couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. The end. Do you want to hear another story?
